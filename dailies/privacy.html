Privacy Policy

effective march 15, 2026

Information We Collect

When you use Dailies, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: Username, first name, email address, and profile picture.
  • Content you create: Posts (responses to dailies), comments, and likes.
  • Phone number hash: If you choose to enable contact-based friend discovery, we store a one-way hash of your phone number. We never store your actual phone number.
  • Device tokens: For push notification delivery.
  • Usage data: Basic interaction data such as follows, likes, and post timestamps.
  • Feedback: If you submit feedback through the app, we store your message, its category, and the app version.

How We Use Your Data

We use your information to:

  • Provide and operate the Dailies app experience.
  • Display your posts and profile to other users.
  • Send push notifications you have opted in to (likes, comments, follows, daily reminders).
  • Enable friend discovery through phone contacts (opt-in only).

Third-Party Services

Dailies uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Authentication: For secure account creation and sign-in. See Firebase Privacy.
  • Tigris (S3-compatible storage): For storing user-uploaded images.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: For delivering push notifications.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Your username, first name, profile picture, and posts are visible to other Dailies users. Third-party services listed above process data on our behalf under their respective privacy policies.

Your Rights

  • Remove phone number: Remove your phone number hash at any time through Privacy settings, stopping contact-based discovery.
  • Delete your account: Permanently delete your account and all associated data through Privacy settings.
  • Manage notifications: Control which push notifications you receive in Notification settings.

Data Retention

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, your personal information is scrubbed immediately. Soft-deleted posts and comments are retained in a non-attributable state but may be permanently purged in the future.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, you can submit feedback directly through Help & Support in the app, or open an issue on our GitHub repository.
